THE MODEL

The accreditation pathway

A formal review process that converts qualified training into verified academic credit — without altering how you operate.

01

Application

Facilities submit core details about training structure, athlete age ranges, and weekly cadence.

  • Facility + primary contact
  • Sports offered
  • Weekly training schedule
02

Eligibility review

Momentum evaluates whether existing programs meet high school credit standards.

  • Hours and instructional alignment
  • Supervision requirements
  • Documentation readiness
03

Program approval

Approved programs are mapped to accredited coursework and prepared for launch.

  • Credit mapping
  • Verification workflow
  • Reporting format
04

Credit issuance

Upon enrollment and payment, credits are issued through accredited partners.

  • Transcript-ready records
  • Institutional delivery
  • School-facing documentation
05

Ongoing verification

Simple weekly confirmations maintain compliance and audit readiness.

  • Weekly attestations
  • Audit-safe records
  • Continuous eligibility
