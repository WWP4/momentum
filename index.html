FAQ

Straight answers parents care about.

Is this extra training or extra work for my athlete?
No. Momentum is designed to recognize training your athlete is already completing. We validate the structure and handle the documentation process.
How long does approval usually take?
Once we have your application and training details, the evaluation step is typically fast. We’ll communicate timing clearly after you submit.
What if my athlete plays multiple sports?
Submit the primary sport and include the rest in the notes. We’ll guide you on the cleanest way to document the full training picture.
What if we are homeschooled or in private school?
Still submit. The process can support different school situations — we’ll confirm the best path during follow-up.
