THE PROCESS

How training becomes credit

A formal review and documentation pathway that turns qualified training into verified high school credit — without changing your athlete’s routine.

01

Family intake

You share the basics about your athlete’s training schedule, season length, and program structure.

  • Sport + level
  • Weekly training schedule
  • Season length + participation
02

Eligibility review

Momentum evaluates whether the training meets high school credit requirements and what documentation is needed.

  • Hours and instructional alignment
  • Supervision / structure requirements
  • Documentation readiness
03

Credit mapping

If eligible, your training is mapped to accredited coursework and prepared for issuance.

  • Course mapping
  • Verification workflow
  • School-facing documentation
04

Credit issuance

After enrollment and payment, credits are issued through accredited partners with transcript-ready records.

  • Transcript-ready records
  • Institutional delivery
  • Verification documentation
05

Ongoing verification

Simple weekly confirmations keep everything compliant and audit-ready throughout the term.

  • Weekly attestations
  • Audit-safe records
  • Continuous eligibility
